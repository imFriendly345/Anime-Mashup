About The Game

Anime Mashup is a new and exciting anime fighting game, with a refreshing combat system that is easy to learn but hard to master. Also including an array of your favorite heroes from your favorite animes, including the likes of one piece, naruto, dragon ball, bleach, death note, chainsaw man, jojo's bizzare adventure, and more! You may also create a custom character to traverse the worlds of all these unique verses and carve your own path to the top! you may also play as your favourite protagonist; From Luffy, Goku, Naruto, ichigo, Tanjiro and Denji!


Different Characters

This game features many characters ranging from main characters like Goku and Naruto and even side characters like Sasuke from Naruto and Zoro from One Piece. With this large aray of characters you can expect to see your favourite characters.
