You Chose Nami

Early Life

Nami is an orphan of war from the Oykot Kingdom. She was found as a baby by Nojiko, her adoptive sister, Then shortly after, a heavily-injured Bell-mère, a female Marine, found the two of them. Despite the ruins and bloodshed she was found in, she was not heavily injured. Bell-mère took Nami and Nojiko back with her to Cocoyasi Village, on the east blue on one of the four seas, where she would then adopt the two children. The three became as close as any real family. When Nami was young she loved drawing. Soon she found her dream, which was to draw maps. and later on, draw the entire world. But this dream was cut short when the infamous Arlong pirate came to her peaceful village and raided it as well as , killing Bell-mère, they then found nami's drawings and made her work for them as a cartographer, when she was three long, said that if she raised 100 million berries(the currency of the world). She would be let free and with him and her crew would leave the island.
